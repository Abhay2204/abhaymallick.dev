# Project Memory & Conventions

## Section Numbering
We are maintaining a strict, consistent numbering system across all major sections of the website to create a cohesive editorial/technical aesthetic.

*   `01` - **HeroSection** (Welcome, Introduction)
*   `02` - **AboutSection** (Personal bio and background)
*   `03` - **ExpertiseSection** (Core expertise areas - horizontal scroll)
*   `04` - **SkillsSection** (Technical arsenal/skills - curtain reveal animation)
*   `05` - **TimelineSection** (Work experience timeline)
*   `06` - **ProjectsSection** (Selected works/projects showcase)
*   `07` - **UiUxGallery** (UI/UX design gallery)
*   `08` - **Footer** (Contact and closing information)

*Note: LoadingScreen, ChatWidget, and MobileView do not require numbering as they are persistent/overlay components.*

## Design System Rules
*   **Colors**: Swiss Minimalist. Mostly White/Black, with precise `blue-600` accents.
*   **Typography**: Geist Sans. High contrast between massive display sizes and ultra-small tracked-out uppercase micro-copy.
*   **Structure**: Sections must use rigid grid alignments and thin structural border lines.
